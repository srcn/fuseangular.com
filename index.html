Same Team. Same Standards. Different Scope.

Fuse Angular started as a single admin template and grew into one of the most trusted Angular foundations around. After a decade of building Angular apps across fintech, education, and enterprise, we created BuilderKit: a broader, more flexible toolkit that takes everything Fuse offers and adds landing pages, marketing templates, and a modern design system.

Reach for Fuse when you need a proven admin dashboard foundation. Reach for BuilderKit when you're building a complete product that goes beyond the admin panel.
